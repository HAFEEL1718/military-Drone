Types of Drones and Their Functions

Quadcopter

A quadcopter is a drone with four rotors. It is commonly used for aerial photography, videography, and recreational purposes.

Fixed-Wing Drone

Fixed-wing drones resemble airplanes and are used for surveying, mapping, and agricultural monitoring due to their long flight times.

Hexacopter

Hexacopters have six rotors, offering more stability and power, making them ideal for carrying heavier payloads.

Military Drone

Military drones are designed for surveillance and combat missions. They are equipped with advanced technology for precision and stealth.
